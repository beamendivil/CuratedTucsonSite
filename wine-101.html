Professional Wine Service

Master the art of professional wine service with step-by-step techniques and best practices that will elevate your restaurant's wine program.

Service Steps

1

Present the Bottle

Present the bottle to the host with the label facing them. Confirm it's the correct wine and vintage.

2

Cut the Foil

Cut the foil cleanly below the lip of the bottle. Remove the foil and wipe the neck clean.

3

Remove the Cork

Insert the corkscrew center, twist until one turn remains, and lever out the cork smoothly.

4

Pour the Taste

Pour a small taste for the host, twisting the bottle to prevent dripping.

5

Serve the Table

Serve ladies first, then gentlemen, then the host. Fill glasses 1/3 to 1/2 full.
